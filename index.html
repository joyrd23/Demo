Singer Vehicle Design

Since our inception in 1948, Singer Design has remained steadfast in our commitment to crafting exceptional automobiles that embody the spirit of Porsche's legendary heritage. It all began with a shared passion for the iconic Porsche 911, an automotive icon that has captivated hearts around the world. With a vision to reimagine this timeless classic, we embarked on a journey to create something truly extraordinary.

Our philosophy at Singer Design revolves around the pursuit of perfection. We meticulously handcraft each Singer vehicle, paying homage to the original design principles while incorporating modern engineering and cutting-edge technologies. Every aspect of our creations is carefully considered, from the finest materials to the smallest details, to ensure an unparalleled driving experience.

Throughout the years, our relentless pursuit of automotive excellence has garnered widespread acclaim and admiration. The Singer 911 has become synonymous with elegance, performance, and unmatched craftsmanship. Our bespoke vehicles have graced the roads of enthusiasts and collectors worldwide, captivating onlookers with their timeless beauty and exhilarating performance.

As we look towards the future, Singer Design remains dedicated to pushing the boundaries of automotive design and engineering. We continue to refine our craft, embracing innovation while honoring the spirit of the Porsche brand. Each Singer creation is a testament to our unwavering commitment to perfection, preserving the essence of the past while forging a path towards the future of automotive excellence.

Join us as we embark on this extraordinary journey, where the timeless charm of the Porsche 911 meets the relentless pursuit of automotive perfection. Welcome to the world of Singer Design, where dreams become reality and every drive is a work of art.
